Restricted numerical shadow

Introduction

Combining the ideas of the restricted numerical range with the numerical shadow one is led in a natural way to the definition of a restricted numerical shadow, $$ P\^RA(z) \ := \ \int\{R} {\rm d} \mu(\psi) \ \delta\Bigl( z-\langle \psi\|A\|\psi\rangle\Bigr) \ , $$ where $ R$ is a selected subset of ${\Omega}_d$, the set of dimension $d$ pure quantum states.
