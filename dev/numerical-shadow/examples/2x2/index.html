Hermitian matrices

Non-normal matrices

Example 1

The matrix is $$\begin{pmatrix} 0&1
0&0 \end{pmatrix}.$$ The shadow resembles a physical shadow cast by the hollow sphere made of transparent material when illuminated by a light source at infinity.
